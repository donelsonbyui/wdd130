Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to offer high adventure experiences to families and individuals who are looking to make memories while at the same time building trust in each other and enjoying the great outdoors.

Audience

Our target audience is indiviuals who enjoy adventure, excitement, and the opportunity to pit their stamina against nature. Our ideal customer is a man or woman who works hard in their career. They are between the ages of 20 and 50. They enjoy nature and challenge. This customer wants an adrenaline rush as they pit their skills against the power of the whitewater rapids. My website will give them the information they need to learn about different rafting excursions, difficulty levels, gear needed, prices, scedules, vacation pacages. This customer will access my shop via laptop or mobile device.
