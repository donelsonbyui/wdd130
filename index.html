Kentucky

Kentucky is a mid-western state in the US known for horse racing, Fort Knox, and the Corvette Museum. Kentucky is also known as the 'Bluegrass' state and we have one of the largest cave systems in North America-Mammoth Cave. Kentucky shares it's border with Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Missouri, Tennessee, Virginia and West Virginia. Kentucky has many trees, lakes and streams.
